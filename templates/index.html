Interview Preparation

How to behave during an interview

✅ Before the interview

  • ☐ Print copies of your resume
  • ☐ Check the address and interview time
  • ☐ Arrive 10 minutes early
  • ☐ Charge your phone

😊 During the interview

  • ☐ Maintain eye contact
  • ☐ Give a firm but gentle handshake
  • ☐ Listen carefully
  • ☐ Answer honestly and briefly

💬 If it feels difficult

  • ☐ Speak more slowly if needed
  • ☐ Ask for clarification before answering
  • ☐ Be honest about your abilities
  • ☐ Use prepared phrases
